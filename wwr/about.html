About Us?

At La Ceiba Whitewater Rafting our passion is to bring excitement and adventure to the hearts of nature and adrenaline lovers. Founded in 1996, we are proud to be leaders in the rafting industry and provide unforgettable experiences on the most exciting rivers. Our mission is simple: we want you to experience nature in a unique, exciting and safe way.

We offer Safety, Passion for Nature, Professionalism, Fun and Adventure. We can't wait to share our passion with you and give you an experience you will never forget. Join us on an exciting journey through the waters and natural beauty that surrounds us. We hope to see you soon on one of our exciting tours!
